Why are trees important to us?

*Trees are important to us because they are good for the economy and they reduce energy bills. They provide many resources, such as food, to a community. Trees mitigate the Urban Heat Island Effect and store and sequester carbon. They are important for habitat.


- We should plant trees because:-

1) Trees are the basis of sustaining life on earth.

2) Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen by Photosynthesis.

3) They also help in reducing soil erosion by binding the soil to their roots.
